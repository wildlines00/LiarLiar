LIARLIAR

I

THE GAME

Liar's Table is a bluffing card game. Up to 4 players sit at the table. Each round, you play cards face-down and declare what they are. You can tell the truth. You can lie. Nobody knows — until someone calls LIAR.

Get caught lying, or make an incorrect accusation, and you face the revolver. Survive long enough to be the last player alive and you win.

III

HOW A ROUND WORKS

Everyone alive is dealt 5 cards. On your turn, play 1 to 3 cards face-down and declare them, like !play 1,3 kings — that says "slots 1 and 3 from my hand, and I claim they're Kings."

The first claim of the round locks the type. If the opener declares Kings, it's a Kings round — every play after that must also claim Kings. Whether your cards actually are Kings is your business.

After each play, the next player has 15 seconds to decide:

PASS

Say nothing and the play stands. It becomes your turn — play your own cards and keep the round moving.

!LIAR

Call it. The cards flip face-up on stream for everyone to see. Somebody's about to face the gun.

EXAMPLE

IEWER1
Plays 2 cards: !play 2,4 queens — "Two Queens." (They're actually a Queen and an Ace. A lie.)
VIEWER2
Smells blood: !liar
THE TABLE
Cards flip: Queen... Ace. LIAR! Viewer1 faces the revolver.

Emptying your hand makes you SAFE. Once you play your last card (and survive any challenge), you're out of the round — you can't be challenged, and you can't challenge anyone. The round continues without you.

Here's the trap: when everyone else goes safe, the last player still holding cards faces the revolver automatically. No reveal, no challenge — just the gun. Dump your cards fast, or die slow.

V

WINNING

Every round, exactly one player pulls the trigger — either from a failed challenge or from being the last one holding cards. Rounds repeat, bullets accumulate, players fall.

The last player alive takes the table. That's it. No points, no second place. Survive.
